Sofiane Affes

My Name is Sofiane Affes and I am studying electrical engineering in Munich, Germany.

Art always played an important role in my life. I joined a painting atelier at the age of six and have been on a creative journey since. Painting, drawing, architecture and, since spring 2017, photography. An inspiring encounter in Madrid triggered me to buy a camera and started this adventure. This is been a lot of fun so far...

Lately I focused more on portraits because of its diversity and the interactive aspect.

I built this website to share it with the world.
